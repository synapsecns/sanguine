# Products

A high-level overview of the products that live under the Cortex / Synapse umbrella.

- **[Synapse Protocol](https://synapseprotocol.com)** – An interchain bridge and messaging layer that lets users and applications move assets and data across EVM and non-EVM chains.

- **[Cortex Protocol](https://cortexprotocol.com)** – An AI-powered DeFi agent and agent platform that executes complex on-chain trading and portfolio operations through natural language across multiple chains.

- **[Hypercall](https://hypercall.xyz/)** – A coming-soon on-chain options venue built on Hyperliquid, focused on offering crypto options with deep liquidity and portfolio-style margining.
